Cushion

One number. How much you can safely spend.

Cushion is a calm money cushion for iPhone. Enter your balances, your bills, and your next payday once. Cushion shows you a single, confident number — how much is genuinely safe to spend before the next paycheck lands — and a plain-language sentence that explains it. No dashboards, no charts, no red warnings.

Everything stays on your iPhone. No bank login, no account, no analytics. Apple's on-device Foundation Models phrase the sentence and read pasted lists; older devices use a built-in static mode. No data ever leaves the device.
