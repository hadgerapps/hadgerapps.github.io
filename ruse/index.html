Ruse.

A clever little puzzle a day — composed on your iPhone, and yours alone.

Open Ruse and today's puzzle is already there: a single, freshly-composed logic puzzle, never repeating. You solve it by pure deduction. The moment you crack the key step, it clicks quietly into place and the trick of it is revealed. Then you're done for the day — no counter to protect, no notification chasing you back.

What makes it different

  • One fresh puzzle a day, invented on your phone — never a re-run, never a download.
  • No streak, no leaderboard, no XP, no badges. What keeps you coming back is the puzzle, not a number you're afraid to break.
  • Fully offline. No account, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking. Nothing leaves your phone.
  • Every puzzle is checked for solvability before you ever see it — always fair, always exactly one answer.

Free, and paid

Today's puzzle is free, forever — the full solve and the full reveal, every day. A subscription or one-time unlock adds the archive of every past puzzle, all theme packs, difficulty selection, and staged hints.
