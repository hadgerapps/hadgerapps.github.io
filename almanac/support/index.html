Common questions

My pattern hasn't appeared yet

Almanac needs at least 7 days of logs and at least 3 days in both the "no drinks" group and the "2 or more drinks" group before it can draw a conclusion. Keep logging your morning check-in and drinks each day. The "Day N of learning" progress count on your Today screen tracks exactly where you are.

I want to cancel my subscription

iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Almanac → Cancel Subscription. Refund requests are handled by Apple; Almanac cannot issue refunds directly.

I deleted the app and lost my data

Almanac stores all data locally on your iPhone. Deleting the app removes all data; there is no server backup. If you reinstall, your subscription is restored via Apple's StoreKit, but drink logs and check-ins cannot be recovered unless you had previously exported them via Settings → Export my data.

I want to delete all my data without uninstalling

Go to Almanac → Settings → Delete all my data. This permanently removes all drinks, check-ins, and your profile from the device. Your subscription (if active) remains in your Apple ID and must be cancelled separately in iOS Settings.

Restore Purchases

Go to Almanac → Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. This re-verifies your active subscription with Apple and restores access if the app was reinstalled.

Privacy and data

Almanac collects nothing and sends nothing anywhere. All data is on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

If you need help beyond what Almanac can offer

Almanac is a personal data tool, not a substitute for professional support. If you're concerned about your alcohol use or your health, please reach out to a healthcare provider or one of these resources:

  • SAMHSA National Helpline (US): 1-800-662-HELP (1-800-662-4357) — free, confidential, 24/7 treatment referral and information service.
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (US): call or text 988 — for people in distress, including substance use crises.
  • NHS Alcohol Support (UK): nhs.uk/live-well/alcohol-advice/alcohol-support/
