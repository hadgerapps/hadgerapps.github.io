Privacy Policy

Effective 15 July 2026.

Everything stays on your iPhone

Vial collects no data. Your wardrobe, photos, wear history, and notes live only in the app's local storage on your iPhone. Label scanning runs on your device with on-device Vision; nothing you photograph or type is uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

The only network call is the weather

To tune the daily "what to wear" pick, Vial reads the current weather through Apple's WeatherKit. It sends no information about you or your collection. Everything else, including the whole core loop, works in Airplane Mode.

No account, no analytics, no tracking

There is no sign-up and no account. Vial contains no analytics or advertising SDKs and does not track you. Our App Privacy label is "Data Not Collected."

Sharing is always your choice

A collection list or a shareable card image leaves your phone only when you export it yourself through the iOS share sheet. Vial never sends anything on its own.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the one-time purchase are processed by Apple. We receive no payment details. Apple's handling is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Your data is deletable

Any bottle or note can be removed in the app at any time; deleting the app deletes everything it stored.
