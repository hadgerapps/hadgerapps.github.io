What Culprit is — and is not

Culprit is a personal skincare diary. You log the products on your shelf and a short daily skin-reaction note, and after about a week Culprit reflects back a read from your own data — which product appears most often on the days your skin reacted. Culprit is a cosmetic product-reaction tracker, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or name any skin condition, and its suspect read is an observation about a product, not advice. Do not rely on Culprit as a substitute for professional medical care; talk to a dermatologist about your skin.
