Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-03 · effective 2026-06-03 · hadger.support@gmail.com

Cushion is built and operated by Hadger studio (IP Shmigirilov, Kazakhstan). This policy describes how Cushion handles your data. The short version: Cushion collects nothing.

What Cushion does not collect

  • No account, no email, no phone number.
  • No bank login, no financial-data aggregator, no third-party payment SDK.
  • No analytics, no third-party SDKs, no telemetry.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA), no cross-app tracking.
  • No location, no microphone, no camera, no contacts, no photo library.

What stays on your iPhone

The balances, bills, payday date, and safety buffer you enter are stored locally on your device via Apple's SwiftData framework, protected by full-device encryption. When you paste a list of transactions, Apple's on-device Foundation Models read it on your iPhone; older devices use a built-in rule-based parser instead. Either way, the text is processed locally and never sent to any server. Cushion does not offer financial advice and is not a regulated financial service.

What network calls Cushion makes

Cushion uses Apple's StoreKit framework for subscription and purchase verification. When you subscribe, buy the lifetime unlock, or restore purchases, the StoreKit framework contacts Apple's App Store endpoints. This is Apple-controlled and unavoidable for any paid app on the App Store. Cushion itself initiates no other outbound network call.

Children

Cushion is rated 4+ but is not designed for children. Cushion does not knowingly collect data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected in this page's "Last updated" date. If a change affects what Cushion collects, the change will be announced in-app before it takes effect.
