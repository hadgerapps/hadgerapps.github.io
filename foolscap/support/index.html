Common questions

How do cryptic clues work? Each clue has a straight definition and a piece of wordplay — an anagram, a hidden word, or a charade — that both lead to the same answer. Solve a rung and Foolscap shows you the wordplay, so the tricks become familiar.

Are the puzzles really different every day? Yes. Each day's ladder is composed fresh on your device and never repeats.

How do I restore a purchase? Open Settings inside Foolscap and tap "Restore Purchases."

How do I cancel Foolscap Plus? Manage or cancel any time in your Apple Account settings — Settings app → your name → Subscriptions.

Do you collect my data? No. Everything happens on your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy.
