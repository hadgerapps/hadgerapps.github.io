Wake

Your sleep, explained.

Wake reads what your Apple Watch already wrote to HealthKit and answers the only question that matters in the morning: why do you feel like this today? Two sentences. On-device. Nothing recorded.

What Wake reads

Sleep stages, heart rate, HRV, resting heart rate — five HealthKit fields. Read-only. Your 30-day baseline is computed and stored locally.

What Wake never does

Record audio. Send your sleep data anywhere. Streak you, badge you, or notify you more than once a day.

Wake's explanation runs entirely on your iPhone via Apple Intelligence (FoundationModels). There is no account, no server, no cloud. Apple Watch required.
