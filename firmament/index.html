Firmament

The sky over the place and day that mattered — computed on your iPhone, kept as a plate.

Pick a place and a date, past or future. Firmament draws the real sky over that spot at that minute — sun, moon, twilight, and the bright stars — as a quiet, museum-grade plate you keep, gift, or share. Everything is computed on your device. Nothing is collected; nothing leaves your phone.
