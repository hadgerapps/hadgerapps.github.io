What Sift is — and is not

Sift is a personal worry-sorting and journaling tool. You write down what's on your mind, and Sift sorts it into Act, Noise, and Recurring — and over time surfaces themes that keep coming back. Sift is a calm sorting tool, not therapy and not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or claim to reduce anxiety, stress, or any condition, and its sorted lists and insights are observations about your own writing, not advice.
