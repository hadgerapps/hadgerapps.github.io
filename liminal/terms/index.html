Liminal Terms of Use

Liminal is a personal creative utility for iPhone and iPad, provided as-is for your own use.

What Liminal makes

Liminal transforms photos you capture or pick into stylized artwork on your device. The output is stylized and interpretive by design; it is not a photographic record and makes no claims of accuracy or fidelity. Style treatments derived from public-domain artworks are listed in the app's About screen.

Your content

The photos you transform and the portals you create are yours and stay on your device. Liminal claims no rights over them. You are responsible for what you capture and for anything you choose to share; respect the rights and privacy of people and places you photograph.

Purchases

Liminal Premium is offered as an auto-renewing monthly ($4.99) or annual ($39.99, 7-day free trial for new subscribers) subscription, billed through your Apple ID. Subscriptions renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends; manage them in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. The free tier remains functional regardless of any purchase.
