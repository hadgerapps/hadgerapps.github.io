Common questions

What is Aplomb? A live rehearsal meter for the talk that matters — an interview answer, a toast, a pitch. Pick the moment, talk, and watch your pace, pauses, and filler words on a calm meter. When you stop, you get one honest page.

Is my audio recorded? No. Speech is analyzed live on your iPhone by Apple's on-device Speech frameworks. The audio is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere — Aplomb works identically in Airplane Mode.

Is this therapy? No. Aplomb rehearses a skill. It is not therapy, speech therapy, or medical advice.

How do I restore a purchase? Open Settings inside Aplomb and tap "Restore Purchases."

How do I delete my data? Open Settings inside Aplomb and tap "Delete all rehearsal data."

Do you collect my data? No. Everything happens on your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy.
