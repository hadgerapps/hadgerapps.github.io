Privacy Policy

Last updated 1 July 2026.

Lila does not collect any data. Every reading is composed and checked entirely on your iPhone. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics, and no tracking.

What stays on your device

Your Intentions, your journey path, and your own written answers are stored only on your device, in the app's own storage (your "Journal of Journeys"). They are never transmitted anywhere and are removed if you delete the app.

What we do not do

We do not collect personal information, contacts, location, photos, microphone, or usage analytics. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not sell or share any information, because we do not collect any.

On-device AI

Where your iPhone supports it, Lila uses Apple's on-device FoundationModels to personalise a reading to your Intention. This processing happens entirely on your device; nothing is sent to Apple, to Hadger, or to any other party.

Crisis-deflection

If Lila detects language suggesting you may be in crisis, it pauses the game and shows external help resources. This check runs entirely on your device and is never logged, stored, or transmitted.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Lila never sees your payment details.

Children

Lila is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.
