Coming to the App Store

A perfume collection organizer that never phones home

Add bottles by scanning the label with on-device Vision, or by hand. Vial catalogs each fragrance with its house, concentration, size, batch code, and its note pyramid (its top, heart, and base notes), so your whole collection becomes a real, searchable perfume inventory. Nothing you scan or type leaves the phone, and Vial's App Privacy label reads "Data Not Collected."

A daily "what should I wear today" pick

Every morning Vial suggests a fragrance to wear, tuned to the season, the occasion, and which scents you haven't reached for lately, a gentle scent tracker for your rotation that gives a one-line reason for each pick. Turn on weather (opt-in) and the pick reads the day's temperature too. Ask Siri or your Action Button "what should I wear today" and Vial answers without opening the app. Everything runs on your device.

Never re-buy a near-duplicate

Standing in a shop, run a quick check and Vial tells you if you already own something close, comparing notes and house against your own cologne collection. One avoided duplicate purchase pays for the app.

Browse your collection by note family

See your wardrobe laid out in a lamplit vitrine, filtered by fragrance notes, season, or occasion. Every niche, decant, or vintage bottle has its place, because no proprietary database decides what counts. The collection is yours to keep and shape.

Private by design

  • No account, no login, no sync, no social feed. Your wardrobe, photos, and wear history live only on your iPhone.
  • Label scanning and the daily pick run entirely on-device. WeatherKit is the only network call, used to tune the pick, and it sends nothing about you or your collection.
  • App Privacy: Data Not Collected. No analytics or advertising SDKs. The full core loop works in Airplane Mode.
  • No ads, no streaks, no badges. A calm, private place for a collection you care about.

Questions

Does Vial need an account?
No. There is no sign-up and no login. You open the app and start adding bottles.
Is my collection private?
Yes, it is on-device. Your wardrobe, photos, notes, and wear history are stored only on your iPhone and are never uploaded. App Privacy: Data Not Collected.
What is a fragrance wardrobe?
It is a fragrance collector's word for the set of perfumes and colognes they own and choose between, their "rotation." Vial is a private organizer for that wardrobe, plus a daily pick from it.
Does the label scan need the internet?
No. Scanning reads the bottle label with on-device Vision. The only network call Vial can make is an opt-in weather lookup for the daily pick, and it sends nothing about you.
How does the daily pick work?
Vial scores each fragrance in your collection for today against the season, the occasion, rotation freshness, and the weather when you opt in, then explains the choice in one line. The scoring runs entirely on your device.
What does it cost?
Free for up to ten bottles with the scan, duplicate check, and a basic pick. Vial Pro is $4.99/week, $29.99/year with a 7-day free trial, or $79.99 once for Lifetime.
