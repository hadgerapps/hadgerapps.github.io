What Meridian is — and is not

Meridian is an educational reference: it summarizes published clinical guidance on perimenopause, with citations and evidence grades, for your personal use. It is not medical advice, it does not diagnose or treat any condition, and it never provides dosing. Content can lag new research; always discuss decisions about your health with a qualified clinician. If a symptom is severe, sudden, or worrying, seek medical care promptly.
