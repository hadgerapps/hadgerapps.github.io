Common questions

The scan missed a line or a quantity. Recognition is imperfect, especially on handwriting — that's why every capture opens in Proofread first. Tap any line to fix it; the original photo is always kept for reference.

Where are my recipes stored? Only on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded. Use Settings → Export all recipes to back your box up as portable files.

How do I restore my purchase? Settings → Restore purchases, signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel the subscription? iPhone Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Trivet Plus. Your recipes remain fully accessible either way.
