Privacy Policy

Effective 11 July 2026.

Everything stays on your iPhone

Meridian collects no data. Its reference library ships inside the app, searches run on your device (including Apple Intelligence matching on supported devices), and the only things Meridian stores are your bookmarks and optional notes — in the app's local storage on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded, transmitted, or shared; Meridian makes no network connections of its own, requests no permissions, and works in Airplane Mode.

No account, no analytics, no tracking

There is no sign-up and no account. Meridian contains no analytics or advertising SDKs and does not track you. Our App Privacy label is "Data Not Collected." Health questions are intimate; we designed Meridian so we structurally cannot see yours.

Sharing is always your choice

The appointment one-pager leaves your phone only when you export it yourself through the iOS share sheet. Meridian never sends anything anywhere on its own.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive no payment details. Apple's handling is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Your data is deletable

Your shortlist and notes can be removed in the app at any time; deleting the app deletes everything it stored.
