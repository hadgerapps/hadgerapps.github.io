What Morsel is — and is not

Morsel is a personal food-and-feeling diary. You log meals and a short daily check-in, and after about two weeks Morsel reflects back patterns in your own data — which foods appear more often on the days you felt a certain way. Morsel is a personal-data mirror, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or test for IBS, food allergy, intolerance, or any condition, and its pattern cards are observations, not advice. Do not rely on Morsel as a substitute for professional medical care; talk to a clinician about symptoms.
