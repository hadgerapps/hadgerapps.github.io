Liminal Support

Questions or bugs? Email hadger.support@gmail.com and a real person will reply.

How do I set a portal as my wallpaper? Tap Set as wallpaper on any portal. Liminal saves it to Photos and shows the two steps: long-press your Lock Screen, then choose the photo. iOS does not let any app change the wallpaper by itself.

The widget shows an old portal? Pick the portal for each widget slot inside the app; widgets refresh on the system's schedule and update shortly after you change the selection.

Why don't I see the Dream styles? The Dream family uses Apple Intelligence and appears only on devices that support it. Every other style works on all iPhones running iOS 26.

Out of transformations for today? The free tier includes three a day, and your existing portals, widgets and wallpapers keep working. Premium removes the limit.

Restore purchases: Settings → Restore Purchases.

Refunds are handled by Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com.
