What Trivet is

Trivet digitizes recipes you photograph, for your personal, private use. You are responsible for what you capture; Trivet provides no recipe marketplace and no redistribution. Trivet is a recipe-organization tool — it provides no nutrition, dietary, or medical advice.

Trivet Plus

The annual plan ($24.99/year, 7-day free trial for new subscribers) is an auto-renewing subscription: payment is charged to your Apple ID; the subscription renews at the same price unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the period ends; manage it in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. The lifetime unlock ($49.99) is a one-time purchase and never renews. Your captured recipes remain viewable, editable, printable, and exportable without any purchase.
