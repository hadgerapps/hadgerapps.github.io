Common questions

What is Lila? A private, on-device adaptation of Leela, the traditional 72-square board game of self-knowledge. Name an Intention and walk the board with an honest Mirror.

What's the difference between Gentle and Unsparing? Both are honest. Gentle is supportive in tone; Unsparing is direct and unflinching. Neither is ever cruel, and you choose every time.

Is this therapy? No. Lila is a contemplative game, not a substitute for professional support.

How do I restore a purchase? Open Settings inside Lila and tap "Restore Purchases."

Do you collect my data? No. Everything happens on your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy.
