Lumen

Your energy, as light.

A daily energy check-in becomes a private light: on-device generated art living on your Home and Lock Screen widgets. Generated on your iPhone. Nothing you feel ever leaves it.

A calm, anti-dopamine mood app. Universal for iPhone and iPad. No account, no notifications, no noise.

The glance, not another streak

Tap two or three mood words, add a line or a photo if you like, and watch today's Lumen bloom into being. One tap sets it across your widgets, and your phone quietly carries today's light — a daily-changing image you glance at, never an app that nags you back.

Made on your iPhone

The daily art is generated on your device — a built-in renderer on every iPhone, with additional Apple Intelligence styles on supported devices. It works in airplane mode. Sharing happens only through the system share sheet, by your hand.

Private by construction

  • No account, no login, no server. Your check-in is processed on your device and never leaves it.
  • App Privacy label: Data Not Collected. The only network call is App Store purchases.
  • No ads, no streaks, no badges, no notification spam. The widget is the point.

Pricing

Questions

Does Lumen need an account?
No. There is no sign-up and no login.
Does it work offline?
Yes. The daily art is generated on the device. Only the App Store needs a connection.
Do I need an Apple Intelligence iPhone?
No. Every iPhone generates your light with a built-in renderer; Apple Intelligence adds extra styles on supported hardware.
Is this a mood tracker or health app?
No. Lumen is reflection and art — it makes no health, therapy, or diagnosis claims.
